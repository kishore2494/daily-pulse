How to use Daily Pulse

A private, offline tracker for your whole life. Here's a 2-minute tour of everything it does — nothing is uploaded anywhere, it all lives on your phone.

LogTimeHabitsGym CalendarWriteStatsCustomizeYour data
1

Log your day

Your daily 60-second check-in.

  • Rate your mood and energy 1–10, jot sleep and deep-work hours.
  • Tick the habits you did — each builds a streak.
  • Add a quick reflection and journal — tap 🎤 to speak instead of type.
  • Scroll down for the optional Deep log — richer metrics when you want them.
👉 Try: tap a few habits, set your mood, and hit Save. That's a logged day.
2

Track your time ⭐

The signature feature — see where your 24 hours actually go.

  • Tap an activity (Work, Travel, Eating…) to start its timer.
  • Tap another to switch instantly — the old one stops itself.
  • Your day fills in as a colored 24-hour timeline you can tap to fix.
  • Forgot to track? Add a block by hand any time.
👉 Try: tap Travel when you leave home, Work when you arrive — watch the timeline build.
3

Habits & streaks

Consistency at a glance.

  • Every habit shows a 🔥 streak and a 90-day heatmap.
  • Green squares are days you did it — keep the chain unbroken.
  • The percentage shows your last-30-day consistency.
4

Gym

A full workout companion.

  • A 6-day split — tap a day to see its exercises.
  • Each move has an animated demo, form tip, and a spot to log reps/weight.
  • Tick exercises off; your gym streak and volume feed into Stats.
👉 Try: don't lift? Hide the whole Gym tab in Customize.
5

Calendar & alarms

Your month, colored by how you felt.

  • Each day is tinted by your mood, with dots for gym, tracked time and events.
  • Add events on any date with a loud alarm — even full-screen over your lock screen.
  • Tap a day to see its summary or jump into its log.
6

Write

Your inner blog — journals, notes, plans in long form.

  • Build articles from blocks: headings, text, checklists, bullets.
  • Tap 🎤 to dictate straight into any block.
  • Drag to reorder; everything saves as you type.
7

Stats & insights

What all that tracking adds up to.

  • Trends for mood, energy, sleep and time — pick 7 / 14 / 30 / 90 days.
  • Auto insights like "commute costs you 2h/day" and sleep-vs-mood links.
  • A weekly review and a Polymath Index scoring body, mind, work, learning & discipline.
8

Make it yours

Nothing here is fixed. In More ▸ Customize:

  • Add, rename, recolor, hide or reorder every habit, activity, field and workout.
  • Rearrange or hide the bottom tabs; pick a theme colour.
  • Edit the deep-log sections and the gym split to match your life.
👉 Try: hide anything you don't use — the app shrinks to just what matters to you.
9

Your data & privacy

Private by design — and that has one flip side.

  • Everything lives only on this phone. No account, no cloud, no tracking.
  • So export a backup now and then (More ▸ Your data) — uninstalling erases everything.
  • Set daily reminders with notification or full-screen alarm modes.
