🔒 Daily Pulse — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 July 2026

The short version: your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly set up your own sync. We run no servers, collect nothing, and can't see your data even if we wanted to.

What data the app stores

Everything you enter — habits, moods, journals, time logs, workouts, tasks, notes, plans, events, articles — is stored locally on your device (browser localStorage). We do not operate any server that receives, stores, or processes your personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. The app contains no analytics, no ads, no trackers, no crash reporters, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Optional features that contact the internet

  • Google Sheet sync (optional, off by default): if you paste your own Google Apps Script link, the app sends your entries to your own Google Sheet in your own Google account. That data flow is between your device and your Google account — we are not involved. Governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Background reminders via ntfy (optional, off by default): if you enable this, reminder titles/labels you write (e.g. "Log my day") are sent to the public push service ntfy.sh on a random secret topic so your phone can ring. Only reminder labels are sent — never your journal or tracked data.
  • Fonts: the app loads its typefaces from Google Fonts.

Data deletion

Your data is yours: export a full backup anytime (More ▸ Your data), and delete everything by clearing the app's site data / uninstalling. There is nothing to delete on our side, because we hold nothing.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
